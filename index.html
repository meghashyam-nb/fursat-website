Handwoven

Textiles we use are woven on handloom across different weaver clusters in India. Most are dyed at the yarn stage using natural or azo-free dyes.

Conscious

We are conscious of the realities of the day and our work factors in fair practices — towards the weavers, the environment and our clients.

Made on Order

We put a piece of clothing together after receiving a confirmed order, rather than creating an inventory of standard sizing which often leads to overproduction.

Familiar
