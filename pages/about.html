Our Story

Handwoven. Slow made. Made with care.

At Fursat Garments, we are dedicated to the art of Indian handwoven textiles, a craft that embodies both heritage and innovation. Founded in 2025, our mission is to create a collection of clothing that resonates with individuals who appreciate the intricate stories woven into every piece. Our offerings include elegant dresses, trendy shirts, and comfortable short pants, all designed to elevate your wardrobe for any occasion.

We adhere to the philosophy of slow living, ensuring that each garment is handspun and nurtured with care. This meticulous process allows us to maintain a commitment to quality, with over 80% of our textiles sourced from local artisans who are masters of their craft. By connecting ancient artistry with modern design, we create timeless pieces that not only inspire comfort but also reflect global elegance.

Our garments are more than just clothing; they are a celebration of craftsmanship and a testament to the dedication of our Karigars. We believe that every thread tells a story, and through our collections, we invite you to embrace the moments of Fursat — those precious times that deserve to be cherished. Join us in honoring this rich tradition while enjoying contemporary styles that cater to the fashion-forward individual.
