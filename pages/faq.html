Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Standard delivery takes 4–7 business days across India. Since most pieces are made to order, please allow 1–3 additional business days for processing before dispatch. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

Yes — all our textiles are woven on handlooms by skilled weavers across India. We work directly with weaver clusters and artisan communities, ensuring fair wages and preserving traditional techniques. Each piece carries the mark of human hands.

We have a detailed Size Guide with measurements for all our garments. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a relaxed, effortless fit — which is how most of our pieces are intended to be worn. You can also WhatsApp us and we'll help you choose.

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery, provided the garment is unworn, with tags intact, and in original packaging. For exchanges, please return the original piece and place a new order. See our full Return Policy.

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹3,000 within India. For orders below ₹3,000, a flat ₹150 shipping fee applies.

Handwoven fabrics need gentle care. We recommend hand washing in cold water with a mild detergent, or dry cleaning for delicate pieces. Avoid wringing — gently press out water and dry flat in shade. See our full Care Guide.

Yes! We love doing custom pieces. WhatsApp us at +91 97392 83637 with your measurements and what you have in mind — whether it's a custom size, a colour variation, or a bespoke piece entirely.

Once your order is dispatched, we'll send a tracking link via WhatsApp and email. You can also reach us anytime at labelfursat@gmail.com or WhatsApp for a quick update.
